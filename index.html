My Vission

Creating A State
For Every Citizen

At the moment in Ondo State, democracy and wide participation by the people have given way to unsolicited and illegitimate outsourcing of power, fascism and totalitarianism.

It is our resolve that political power that is based on participation by the people must go back where it rightly belongs – the people.

The Agboola Ajayi governorship shall return power and participation in government and political leadership to the people of Ondo State from Akotogbo to Akunnu. Open politics and effective leadership must secure the wellbeing of the residents of the state.
